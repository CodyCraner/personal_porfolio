Home page

Why will people come to your site?

The purpose of the site is to sell me to employers and also offer ideas as well as examples of coding concepts for those interested in coding. This website is to be a tool for others to see what my career path is at the moment, my goals and all pertaining facts about me! People will come to understand where I come from and my process to make projects. Overall people will want to see what I have created, what I aspire to become and all topics relating to me as a person.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

The website will help people who wonder what I have worked on, am working on and will work on in relation to Computer Science as a whole. This site will be a way to learn about my character, work and all sorts of information about Cody Craner. This will also be a good way to learn about starting a path in the field of Computer Science or Cyber Security for those who are interested in starting, for they can see the steps I took in order to develop the portfolio and start my career or education.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

This is my personal portfolio, those that visit will likely be looking at me specifically for work or related thing as well as looking to see a set career path in Computer Science or Cyber Security. My site will be clear and consise to help the goals of those looking for employees or a website mentor. With all this in mind my website will show great projects and help those interested in me, making them look over my website over others to see what value it holds pertaining to the process in which they seek. The multifaceted approach will allow more people to traffic the site instead of a simple stale focus on one singular point of me or coding.

What is the website have to make it friendly to a wide demographic?

No matter who is taking interest in me and the projects all things will be explained or displayed in a way for all to understand. The goal is to present my accomplishments for all, not just using computer science wording or other concepts that all may not be familiar with. Some content such as code or naming conventions will be different for some, the context and explination will help it be more clear for the generasl public who will be visiting the websites pages.
